Our Story

Transforming lives through education since 2020

Who We Are

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education to underprivileged children in urban slums and rural areas across India. Founded in 2020, we believe education is the most powerful tool to break the cycle of poverty.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create accessible learning opportunities for every child, regardless of their socioeconomic background. We focus on holistic development that includes academic education, life skills, and values to help children become confident, responsible citizens.

Our Approach

We implement innovative teaching methods tailored to community needs. Our programs include:

  • Community-based learning centers
  • Digital literacy programs
  • Vocational training for older students
  • Parental awareness workshops
